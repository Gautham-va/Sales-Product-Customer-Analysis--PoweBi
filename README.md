This project presents a multi-page Power BI dashboard built as part of a data analyst assessment using a sample dataset.
The goal was to transform raw sales data into clear, actionable insights by combining strong data modelling, DAX measures, and dashboard design.

The report delivers a structured view of Cavitak‚Äôs overall performance, product trends, and customer behaviour.

##üîç Key Insights
###Overall Performance

Cavitak generated 200M in revenue and 19.16M in profit, with sales reaching their highest point in August before declining later.

Major revenue contributions came from Kolkata, Bangalore, Chennai, Ahmedabad, and Pune.

Products like Printers and Monitors delivered strong margins, while Laptops showed lower profitability despite high sales volume.

###Product Insights

Printers were the top-performing product in both revenue and profit.

The Accessories and Computers categories accounted for the largest share of overall sales.

Profit dipped during March and June, followed by a recovery toward the later months.

Several products had high sales but lower margins, revealing opportunities for pricing or cost optimisation.

###Customer Insights

Cavitak served 55 active customers, with Customer_53 contributing the highest revenue and purchase volume.

A small group of customers generated a large share of total revenue, showing dependence on high-value clients.

Cities such as Ahmedabad, Kolkata, Pune, and Bangalore had the highest number of active customers.
